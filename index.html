About Karthick Raja

Karthick Raja is a passionate instructor with expertise in both traditional yoga and ancient Indian martial arts. His teaching includes the wisdom of traditional yoga and the discipline of Adimurai and Kalari.

Teaching Philosophy

At Aayakalai, we believe in preserving and sharing the authentic traditions of Indian arts. Our approach emphasizes discipline, respect, and the harmonious development of body, mind, and spirit.

Credentials

  • Former physical education instructor
  • M.Sc (Therapeutic Yoga)
  • RYT-500 Certified Yoga Teacher
  • Certified in Yoga Therapy
  • Master of Adimurai and Kalari traditions
  • Workshops in India and abroad

Our Classes

Traditional Yoga

Comprehensive yoga classes covering Asana (postures), Pranayama (breath control), Meditation, Therapeutic Yoga, Physical Exercise, Cardio, and General Strengthening. Perfect for all levels.

Kalari/Silambam

Learn the ancient Tamil martial art of Kalari and Silambam - an entire system of self-defense, weapon training (swords, spears, sticks), and physical conditioning. Open to all.
