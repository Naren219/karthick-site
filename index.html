About Karthick

Meet Your Teacher

Karthick is a passionate and certified yoga instructor with over 10 years of experience. His teaching blends traditional Hatha and Vinyasa yoga, focusing on mindful movement, breath, and holistic well-being.

Teaching Style

Karthick's classes are welcoming, supportive, and tailored to each student's needs. He believes yoga is for everyone and strives to create a safe, inclusive space for all.

Credentials

  • Former physical education instructor
  • M.Sc (Therapeutic Yoga)
  • Took yoga therapy course
  • RYT-500 Certified Yoga Teacher
  • Certified in Yoga Therapy
  • 10+ years teaching experience
  • Workshops in India and abroad
